📅 First Trimester Timeline (Week 1–12)

🤒 Common Symptoms

  • Morning sickness and nausea
  • Fatigue and tiredness
  • Breast tenderness
  • Frequent urination
  • Mood swings

💡 Tips for Mothers

Eat small, frequent meals to reduce nausea.
Get plenty of rest and stay hydrated.
Take prenatal vitamins rich in folic acid.
Avoid alcohol, smoking, and caffeine.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

When should I visit the doctor?
You should schedule your first prenatal visit around 6-8 weeks after your last period.
Is cramping normal in the first trimester?
Mild cramping is common due to the uterus stretching, but severe pain should be checked by a doctor.
Can I continue to exercise?
Yes, light to moderate exercise is beneficial. Avoid strenuous workouts without consulting a doctor.
