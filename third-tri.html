📅 Third Trimester Timeline (Week 28–40)

🤰 Common Symptoms

  • Shortness of breath
  • Swelling in hands, feet, and ankles
  • Heartburn and indigestion
  • Frequent urination and sleep disturbances
  • Braxton Hicks contractions

💡 Tips for the Third Trimester

Prepare your hospital bag early.
Attend childbirth education classes.
Sleep on your left side to improve circulation.
Stay active with light stretching and walking.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What are Braxton Hicks contractions?
They're practice contractions that are usually painless and help your body prepare for labor.
When should I go to the hospital?
Go if you experience regular contractions, your water breaks, or decreased baby movement. Always consult your doctor for exact guidance.
How can I relieve back pain?
Use pillows to support your back while sleeping, maintain good posture, and try prenatal yoga or stretching.
